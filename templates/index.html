This article is about Google's news aggregator. For the former app, see Google News & Weather. For Google's initiative to support journalists, see Google News Lab. For access to newsgroups through Google, see Google Groups. Google News is a news aggregator app developed by Google. It presents a continuous, customizable flow of articles organized from thousands of publishers and magazines. Google News is available as an app on Android, iOS, and the Web. Google released a beta version in September 2002 and the official app in January 2006.[1] The initial idea was developed by Krishna Bharat.[2][3] The service has been described as the worlds largest news aggregator.[4]
